Harry Potter + Paula

I am Harry Potter, and I love Paula deeply. Our story started in 2023 when we were still in high school. I noticed her first in the corridor after prep, smiling like she already knew how my whole day would go.

We became friends through small talks, shared notes, and those long walks after class where time felt slower. Before long, she became the one person I wanted to tell everything to, from my fears to my biggest dreams.

What makes her special is not just beauty, it is her heart. She gives peace, she gives strength, and she makes ordinary days feel like a celebration. Through ups and downs, we kept choosing each other, and that is what love means to me.

This page is my reminder that our beginning was real, our growth is real, and our future is worth fighting for. Paula, if you are reading this: thank you for being my favorite chapter.
